French Fries

This lasagna recipe takes a little work, but it is so satisfying and filling that it's worth it!

Prep Time Cook Time Total Time
15 minutes 10 minutes 25 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 ½ pounds russet potatoes, peeled
  • 1 cup vegetable oil for frying
  • 1 cup all purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon garlic salt
  • 1 teaspoon onion salt
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup water, or as needed

Directions

  1. Step 1:

    2. Slice potatoes into French fries and place them into a large bowl of cold water to prevent them from turning brown.

  2. Step 2:

    3. Heat oil in a heavy saucepan or large skillet to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).

  3. Step 3:

    4. While the oil is heating, sift flour, garlic salt, onion salt, paprika, and salt into a large bowl. Gradually stir in enough water to make a slightly thick batter that can be drizzled from a spoon.

  4. Step 4:

    5. Drain fries and pat dry; dip into the batter one at a time.

  5. Step 5:

    6. Lower fries carefully into the hot oil in batches to prevent them from clumping together. Fry until golden brown and crispy on all sides, about 7 to 10 minutes.

  6. Step 6:

    7. Drain fries on a paper towel-lined plate.

  7. Step 7:

    8. Serve hot and enjoy!
